Spot Hidden Damage

Damage is not always easy to see from the ground. We inspect roofing, siding, gutters, flashing, and other exterior areas to look for storm damage, wear, loose materials, and problem spots that may need attention.

Understand Repair Priorities

Not every issue needs major work right away. We help you understand what can be repaired, what should be watched, and what may need replacement so you can make a smart decision for your home.

Check Attic & Moisture Areas

Some exterior problems show up inside the attic first. We look for signs of leaks, moisture, ventilation issues, and water intrusion that could point to roofing, flashing, or exterior drainage concerns.

Document Storm Damage

If wind, hail, or heavy weather caused damage, clear documentation matters. We can identify visible issues, explain what we find, and provide helpful information if repairs or an insurance claim may be needed.
